Questions

Frequently asked

Is this financial advice?+
No. Argos is an analytical and educational platform. It identifies systematic LONG and SHORT setups based on technical and statistical conditions. It does not recommend buying or selling any asset. All trading decisions are your own responsibility.
Does it trade for me automatically?+
No. Argos monitors markets and identifies setups. It does not connect to any exchange or execute trades on your behalf. You decide what to do with the signals.
Can I download the course?+
No. The course is hosted exclusively on the private Argos platform. Videos cannot be downloaded or screen-recorded. Access requires an active account.
What does 10.5% TP / 5.4% SL mean?+
With 3x leverage: Take Profit is +10.5% PnL (price moves +3.5% in your favor), Stop Loss is −5.4% PnL (price moves −1.8% against you). Fixed levels set from the entry price of each position.
Which exchanges / pairs does it support?+
Argos currently uses Binance market data. You can monitor any active trading pair available on Binance — from BTC/USDT to smaller altcoins.
